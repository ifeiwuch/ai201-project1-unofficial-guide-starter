What are the most prestigious student organizations at Brown University?
I'm thinking about applying to Brown and am curious about the student life there. Are there any organizations or clubs that are especially well-regarded or have a special status on campus?

Sure, here's some information about noteworthy student clubs at Brown.
1. Brown University Model UN: This organization is one of the most prestigious on campus, as it often competes (and places well) in national and international conferences.
2. The Brown Daily Herald: This independent newspaper has been continuously published since 1891 and provides students with an opportunity to develop their journalism skills.
3. Brown Political Review: This student-run publication is widely read and respected for its thoughtful, well-researched articles on a wide range of political topics.
4. Brown Entrepreneurs Program (EP): This is known for organizing multiple successful initiatives and events every year to promote innovation and entrepreneurship on campus.
5. Brown Debating Union: This club is highly respected because it participates in competitive collegiate debate tournaments around the country.
6. Brown University Science Olympiad (BUSO): This club organizes one of the largest Science Olympiad tournaments in the Northeast, which attracts high school teams from numerous states.
7. Brown University Gilbert and Sullivan (BUGS): This performing arts group is highly regarded and puts on a musical theatre production every semester, often with a modern and creative twist.
Remember that the best club or organization for you will align with your interests and goals. Brown prides itself on having over 400 registered student organizations, so there is truly something for everyone.
